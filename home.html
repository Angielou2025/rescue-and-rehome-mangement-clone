Rescue and Rehome Management system for pet

The goal of animal shelters is to save lives and prevent suffering. We welcome stray or abandoned animals and we provide them with veterinary attention, food, water and shelter and we find a new home for them.

In order to adopt one of our dogs we have a process: we visit the new home and ask for a small payment for the adoption.

Our intention is to be 100% sure that these animals receive the second chance they deserve.
